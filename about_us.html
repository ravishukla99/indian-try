About Us

‘INDIAN SCIENTIFIC’ is the world’s most leading consulting firm, a reliable service provider occupied in offering PRODUCT TESTING LABORATORY & ISI /BIS CERTIFICATION, FOOD TESTING LABORATORY & FOOD LICENSE CERTIFICATION, TRAINING, REGULATORY COMPLIANCES, INDIAN STANDARD ASSESSMENT like ISI Certification Services, Foreign Manufacturer Certification, NABL Accreditation Consultancy, AGMARK Certification, Indian BIS and CRS Scheme for Electronics & IT Goods, and many more. As of now, products certifications & compliances is being catered by ''INDIAN SCIENTIFIC'' The offered range of service is rendered in obedience with the quality constraints by our competent experts who hold a rich understanding of the particular fields.

''INDIAN SCIENTIFIC'' Group was originated in the year 2004 and established in the year 2013. Initially, the first subsidiary INDIAN SCIENTIFIC of the group was selected for product certification for the domestic manufacturers, and the ranges of certification were only three - BIS, & ISI .
